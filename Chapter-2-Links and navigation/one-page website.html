Plot

"Dahmer Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story" is a limited series that delves into the life and crimes of notorious serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer. It follows Dahmer's disturbing journey from his troubled adolescence to his reign of terror in the late 20th century.Dahmer would bring men to his home, drug them, r*** them, and gradually kill them by dismembering their body parts. When Dahmer murdered his second victim, he was in a hotel room. He cut up the body, placed it in a suitcase, and returned to his granny's house."

Cast

The main cast of "Dahmer Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story" includes Evan Peters, Niecy Nash, Penelope Ann Miller, Richard Jenkins, Shaun J. Brown, and Colin Ford.
